## Key Exam Topics

The Databricks Data Engineer Professional certification covers:

- Delta Lake operations and optimization
- Data pipeline design and orchestration with Databricks Workflows
- Data governance with Unity Catalog
- Performance tuning and query optimization
- Structured Streaming
- Change Data Capture (CDC) patterns
- Multi-hop architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold)
