# Mock Exam 2 Debrief — ML Associate

After timing yourself through this mock, use this debrief to:

1. **Identify weak domains** with the per-question map below
2. **Find the topic folder** that covers each missed question
3. **Follow the study plan** matched to your miss count

> [!tip]
> The number of questions you missed matters less than *which domains* they came from. Three misses on a 5 %-weight domain hits differently than three misses on a 22 %-weight domain.

## Study plan by miss count

| Missed | Status | Study plan |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| **0–6** (≥ 85 %) | Excellent | Quick review of any missed topic; you're exam-ready |
| **7–13** (70–84 %) | Likely passing | Identify the 2-3 weakest domains; spend 1-2 hours per domain re-reading the topic folder |
| **14–20** (55–69 %) | On the bubble | Block 1-2 weeks of focused study on the weakest domains; re-attempt the other mock |
| **21–30** (40–54 %) | Not ready yet | 2-3 weeks of comprehensive review; work through each topic folder in order |
| **31+** (< 40 %) | Significant gap | Restart from shared/fundamentals/. Plan a 4-6 week cycle |
