## How to Take This Exam

1. Set a 90-minute timer before opening [questions.md](./questions.md)
2. Answer all questions without referring to notes or documentation
3. Flag any questions you are unsure about
4. When time is up, review your answers
5. Grade yourself: count correct answers and calculate your percentage
6. For any incorrect answers, review the explanation and the relevant topic study material

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Result | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 36–45 (80–100%) | Excellent — ready to sit the exam | Schedule your exam |
| 32–35 (71–79%) | Passing — but review weak areas | Review missed topics, attempt Mock Exam 2 |
| 28–31 (62–70%) | Close — focused study needed | Study weak domains, retry in 1 week |

## Tips for Taking Mock Exams

- Read each question fully before looking at the answer choices
- Eliminate obviously wrong answers to narrow your options
- Watch for wording like "BEST", "MOST", "FIRST" — the exam tests nuanced judgment
- Do not spend more than 2 minutes on any single question
- If unsure, make your best guess and flag for review — do not leave blanks
